The Nightlife.

Hey, there are so many fantastic places to have a great time in Panama City! To help you out,
I've put together a list of 10 of the most popular entertainment and nightlife spots.

Whether you're looking for a fun night out at a bar or nightclub, or just want to enjoy some live music
or unique atmosphere, there's sure to be something on this list that you'll love.

So, let's dive in and get ready for an unforgettable experience!
We will take you to...

Hard Rock Hotel

Panama city, Megapolis Tower 1, Panama City

For those looking for a high-energy night out in Panama City, the Hard Rock Hotel is the perfect destination. This hotel boasts a variety of entertainment options, including a casino, multiple bars, and a nightclub that stays open until the early hours of the morning.

Whether you want to test your luck at the slots, enjoy a drink with friends, or dance the night away, the Hard Rock Hotel has it all. The hotel's rock-and-roll theme adds to the fun atmosphere, making it a must-visit spot for anyone looking to let loose and have a great time in Panama City.

Calle Uruguay

Panama city, Calle Uruguay

If you're looking for a lively night out in Panama City, Calle Uruguay is the place to be. This bustling street is famous for its abundance of bars and nightclubs, drawing in locals and tourists alike. From small, cozy bars to large, pulsating clubs, Calle Uruguay has something for everyone.

The street's vibrant atmosphere and diverse entertainment options make it a must-visit destination for anyone looking to experience Panama City's nightlife scene. So, gather your friends and head out to Calle Uruguay for a night you won't soon forget.

Teatro Amador

Panama city, Avenida Central within 11th & 13th St.

Teatro Amador, located in the heart of Casco Viejo, is a unique and historic venue that offers an unforgettable nightlife experience. This former theater has been converted into a nightclub, complete with a stage for live music and DJs spinning the latest tracks.

The venue's grand architecture and vintage décor add to the unique atmosphere, providing the perfect backdrop for a night of dancing and music. If you're looking for a one-of-a-kind experience in Panama City, be sure to check out Teatro Amador and its vibrant nightlife scene.

La Rana Dorada

Panama city, (9th St/Calle Boquete)

La Rana Dorada is a popular brewery and pub with multiple locations throughout Panama City. Known for its locally brewed craft beers and lively atmosphere, La Rana Dorada is a favorite among locals and visitors alike. The pub's cozy interior and outdoor seating areas make it a great spot to relax with friends and enjoy some drinks and good conversation.

Whether you're a beer enthusiast or just looking for a fun night out, La Rana Dorada is definitely worth checking out. With its great beer, tasty food, and lively atmosphere, it's a top pick for anyone looking to experience Panama City's nightlife scene.

Havana Panama

Panama city, Banking Zone

Havana Panama is a must-visit destination for anyone looking to experience a taste of Cuba in Panama City. This lively bar boasts a vibrant Cuban-themed atmosphere and is known for its delicious cocktails and live music performances. The venue's cozy interior and outdoor terrace provide the perfect setting for enjoying a night out with friends or a romantic evening with a loved one.

Whether you're a fan of Cuban music, great drinks, or just looking for a unique nightlife experience, Havana Panama is definitely worth checking out. Don't miss out on the chance to dance the night away to some of the best live music in town.

Casa Jaguar

Panama city, Av. Central España, Panamá

Casa Jaguar is a one-of-a-kind bar located in the lush rainforest just outside of Panama City. This outdoor venue offers a unique nightlife experience, with its stunning natural surroundings and laid-back atmosphere.

The bar's open-air design allows visitors to immerse themselves in the beauty of the rainforest while enjoying delicious drinks and great company. Whether you're looking for a romantic evening under the stars or a fun night out with friends, Casa Jaguar is the perfect destination.

So, step into the jungle and enjoy a truly unforgettable night out at this incredible venue.

The Strangers Club

Panama city, Avenida Central y, C. B, Panamá

The Strangers Club is a popular bar in Panama City that's known for its unique and delicious craft cocktails. The bar's skilled mixologists are constantly experimenting with new and interesting ingredients to create innovative and flavorful drinks that are sure to impress.

The venue's lively atmosphere and music selection make it a great spot for a fun night out with friends. So, if you're looking for a unique and exciting nightlife experience in Panama City, be sure to check out The Strangers Club and their amazing selection of craft cocktails. .

La Tana

Panama city, 12st. B, Panama City

La Tana is a hidden gem of a bar located in the heart of Casco Viejo. This unique venue is situated in the basement of a historic building, adding to its underground and secretive vibe. The cozy interior and intimate setting make it a great spot for a romantic evening or a night out with close friends.

La Tana's selection of classic cocktails and wines perfectly complement the bar's unique atmosphere. If you're looking for a truly unique nightlife experience in Panama City, be sure to check out La Tana and its unforgettable ambiance. .

La Buat

Panama city, XF26+5GQ, C. 12 W., Panamá

La Buat is a futuristic nightclub in Panama City that's known for its electronic music and vibrant atmosphere. The club's sleek design and state-of-the-art lighting systems create a high-energy ambiance that draws in a diverse crowd of locals and tourists. br/>
La Buat's resident DJs are some of the best in the city, spinning a variety of electronic music genres to keep the party going all night long. If you're a fan of electronic music and looking for an unforgettable nightlife experience in Panama City, be sure to check out La Buat.

The Wine Bar

Panama city, Plaza Downtown, El Cangrejo, Vía Veneto, Panamá

The Wine Bar, located in the American Trade Hotel in Panama City, is a great spot for those who love fine wine and craft cocktails. The bar has an impressive selection of wines from all over the world, making it a perfect destination for wine enthusiasts.

Along with the extensive wine list, The Wine Bar also serves up delicious craft cocktails that are expertly crafted by the bartenders. The ambiance is cozy and inviting, making it a perfect spot for a romantic evening or a night out with friends. Be sure to check out The Wine Bar if you're in Panama City and in search of a great wine or cocktail experience. .

La Fabrica

Panama city, Calz. de Amador, Panamá

La Fabrica is a popular brewery and restaurant located in Panama City that's known for its fantastic selection of craft beers. The establishment offers a wide range of beers, from classic pilsners to bold stouts, making it an ideal spot for beer enthusiasts. The restaurant also offers a delicious menu of food options, including burgers, sandwiches, and pizzas, that pair perfectly with the brews.

The ambiance is casual and relaxed, making it a great spot to catch up with friends or enjoy a laid-back evening out. If you're a fan of craft beer and good food, La Fabrica is definitely worth checking out.
