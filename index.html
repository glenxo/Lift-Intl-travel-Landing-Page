Tours & Trips

Tubing Los Cajones de Chame

$220.00 for 2 pax, +$30.00 each additional · 4 hours 45 minutes and up.
"The tube" and helmet, the cooler with iced waterbottles and seasonal fresh fruit are included as a courtesy and, client pays +$5.00 fee each ride down the rapids.

Pacific Beaches up to Coronado

One way $110.00 +$10 after 3rd guest or $180.00 +$20 3rd · 6 hours 30 minutes and up.
If you want to go for a short Drive to spend time at quality beaches, this is the right option for you.

Soberanía combo Monkeys, fishing Tour

$390 for 1 or 2 guests, +$55 after 3rd guest · 5 hours 30 minutes and up.
Mix both activities and if you have the interest you can pay the $5 tax for admittance to visit at the San Antonio Embera Embera tribal Village. Included: road and water transportation, cooler with cut up fresh fruit, water and ice, minoes and fishing rods.

Fishing in the Pacific

Starts at $400 1 or 2, $65.00 3rd & on · 4 hours 30 minutes and up.
The Pacific Ocean is a very large eco system, and in Panamá we claim abundance of fish is our name. No doubt you will always catch more than plenty fish with our expert Skippers. Includes: cooler with water and ice, bait and fishing equipment.

Embera Tribe

$130.00 x person · 5 hours 15 minutes and up
This is an authentic Indian Village where the natives will share cultural background, crafts, dance, the beauty of their people and enviorment. Included: fruit Snack with fish and chips, road and water transportation.

City/Canal tour

$100 x1 or 2 guests + $20 after the 3rd · aprox. 4 hours 15 minutes and up.
An average of 4 places in 4 hours, including: Panama Canal (Miraflores locks), Casco Antiguo, Causeway, sites and monuments.

Nightlife, Party Time Exce-llent

$25xhour 1 or 2 guests, $5 after 3rd · 2 hours and up.
#designateddriver for Metropolitan Area Wine and Dine, Clubbing, Bar Hopping, bachelor and bachlorette groups bar hopping fun.

Horse and Waterfall Chepo Guided Tour

$295 for 2 guest for 4x4 tour, add +$55 after 3rd guest · 7 hours 15 minutes and up.
It includes horse ride, lunch and a water bottle, the journey is an extreme 4x4 ride through rivers and jungle.

Panamá Viejo, ruined city founded 1519

$60.00 · 2 hours 30 minutes.
Site of the 1st city founded on the Pacific coast of the new world. // Sitio donde de fundó la primera ciudad en el pacífico del nuevo mundo.

Colón City/Canal w/option for Train Tour

$250 1 or 2 guests, +$20 after 3rd · 6 hours 45 minutes and up.
3rd set of Locks visitor's center (Aguas Claras admitance not included), San Lorenzo ruins, Colon city and Zona Libre perimeter. The only passenger train leaves at 7:15 am from the Corozal train station, tickets not included in the price and we would have to be at the station at least by 7 am.

Soberanía Eco Tour with Fishing

$350 1 or 2 guests, $55.00 3rd guest · 4 hours 45 minutes and up.
Withing Gatun lake and the rainforrest all around it, we have a large population of wildlife and one of the funner activities is fishing a Peacock Bass, Oscar or even invasive tiger fish in the lake. With option to try the "catch of the day" for a small additional fee we can grill it by a waterfall while you enjoy nature. Includes: cut up fresh fruit, water and ice in a cooler, minoes and fishing rods.

Hourly Rates metro area/Exec. Transport.

$18xhr 1 or 2 guests, +$5 after the 3rd · 2 hours and up.
Lift-Intl Proffesional Contracted Drivers (2 hours minimum, language skills and interpreters available with timely request): They are punctual, and easy to track down if you need to get the same driver for any unfinished business or follow up.

El Valle de Anton (Blasted open Volcano)

$250 for 1 or 2 guests, +$20 after 3rd · 7 hours 15 minutes and up.
High grounds make for mild weather, and this is the ideal chill out spot where you can swim under waterfalls, ATV or hike trails, crafts market shopping, zoo, reptiles, butterflies, square bark trees, fresh air (O2).

Isla Grande y Portobelo

$250 for 1 Spend a day on the Caribbean side for swiming in the blue seas of our sandy beaches (option to cross to an Island $6 for boat, for an option of private or public beach areas), and also get your share of Culture as this activity includes a World Heritage Site attraction.

City Tour

$90 1 or 2 guests + $10 after the 3rd · 4 hours and up
Start in Metro Area, 4 hours on average, includes: Spanish Colonial Legacy in San Felipe's Casco Antiguo, Causeway, Monuments and Sites, Ancon Hill.
