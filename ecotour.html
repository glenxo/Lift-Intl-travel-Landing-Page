Knowing the fish species

Panama city, Banking zone, Via espana

Knowing the fish species in Lake Gatun is crucial to a successful fishing trip. The lake is home to a variety of fish species, including peacock bass, snook, tarpon, and tilapia, among others. Different fish species have different feeding patterns and habitats, so it is important to know which species you are targeting and what kind of bait and lures to use.

Peacock bass, for example, are aggressive predators that feed on smaller fish and insects, so using topwater lures or live bait can be effective. On the other hand, tilapia are herbivores that feed on plants and algae, so using soft plastic baits or worms can be more successful. Knowing the fish species in Lake Gatun and their behaviors can greatly increase your chances of a successful fishing trip.
