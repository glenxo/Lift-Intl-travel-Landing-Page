Casco antiguo walking tour

30 x person

A 2 - 3 hour and up tour
Walkaround
through
the historical district of Panama City and its UNESCO World Heritage site.

Start at $30 for 1 or 2 guests +$5 for the 3rd · 2 - 3 hours 15 minutes and up.
Experience the historic and cultural charm of Casco Viejo, Panama on a walking city tour. Stroll through the UNESCO World Heritage Site's narrow streets and visit landmarks such as the Panama Canal Museum and Church of San Francisco.
A knowledgeable guide will provide interesting anecdotes and historical context. Enjoy a truly memorable and enriching experience in this charming neighborhood.
