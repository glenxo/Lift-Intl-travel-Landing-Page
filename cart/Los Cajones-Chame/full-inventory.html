Los Cajones de Chame is a hidden gem located in the Chame district of Panama. Known for its stunning natural beauty, Los Cajones de Chame is a series of narrow gorges and cascading waterfalls that are a must-visit for any nature enthusiast.
The gorges are formed by the Chame River, which runs through a narrow canyon and creates a series of waterfalls and pools that are perfect for swimming and relaxing. The canyon walls rise up to 60 meters in some places, creating a dramatic and awe-inspiring landscape.

Visitors can explore the gorges by hiking or climbing through the narrow canyon, or by swimming and jumping into the natural pools. The area is also popular for rock climbing, with many climbers flocking to the steep canyon walls to test their skills.
Los Cajones de Chame is also home to a diverse range of flora and fauna, including monkeys, birds, and exotic plants. Visitors can take a guided tour of the area to learn about the local wildlife and vegetation, or simply take a relaxing stroll through the lush forested area.
Overall, Los Cajones de Chame is a natural wonder that is not to be missed. Whether you're an adrenaline junkie or simply seeking a peaceful retreat, Los Cajones de Chame offers something for everyone.
