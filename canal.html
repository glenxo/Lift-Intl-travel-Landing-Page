More about this trip



El Chorro Macho Waterfall:
A picturesque waterfall with natural pools for swimming and relaxation.

El Nispero Zoo and Botanical Garden:
A zoo and botanical garden showcasing native wildlife and plants.

Market and Handicrafts Plaza:
A lively market where your customers can find local crafts, fresh produce, and tasty snacks.

La India Dormida Hike:
A moderate hike offering panoramic views of the valley and surrounding mountains.

El Valle Amphibian Conservation Center:
A center dedicated to preserving endangered amphibian species, including the golden frog.

APROVACA Orchid Nursery:
A place to admire a wide variety of stunning orchids native to Panama.

Serpentarium:
An opportunity to see various species of snakes and learn about their importance in the ecosystem.

Canopy Adventures:
Zip-line through the rainforest and enjoy an adrenaline-pumping experience.

El Valle Artisanal Market:
A market where local artisans sell handmade crafts and artworks.

El Macho Volcano:
Take a guided hike to the summit of the dormant volcano for breathtaking views.
