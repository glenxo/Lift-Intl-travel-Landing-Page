El Nispero Zoo

Panama city, Banking zone, Via espana

Located in the heart of El Valle de Anton, the El Nispero Zoo and Botanical Garden is a must-visit for animal and nature enthusiasts. Here you'll have the opportunity to see a variety of species, including jaguars, ocelots, sloths, monkeys, and exotic birds. The zoo also houses a botanical garden, where you can explore the diversity of plants and trees found in Panama.

The El Nispero Zoo and Botanical Garden is a great place to learn about Panama's biodiversity and conservation efforts. You can take a guided tour of the zoo to learn about each animal and their habitats, and visit the greenhouse to see rare and exotic plants. The zoo also has a butterfly house where you can get up close and personal with these beautiful insects.

Don't forget to stop by the gift shop to pick up a souvenir, and bring a camera to capture the stunning views of the zoo and surrounding mountains. A visit to El Nispero Zoo and Botanical Garden is a fun and educational experience for the whole family.

El Chorro Macho waterfall

Panama city, Calle 50

El Valle de Anton in Panama is a paradise for nature lovers, and one of the must-visit places is El Chorro Macho waterfall. The hike to the waterfall is relatively easy, with well-maintained trails, and the lush greenery along the way makes the journey even more enjoyable.

You can take a refreshing dip in the cool waters of the waterfall and enjoy the peaceful surroundings. Another popular activity in El Valle is relaxing in the hot springs at Pozos Termales, known for their therapeutic properties.

The natural mineral-rich water is believed to have healing properties for the skin, muscles, and joints, and it's a great way to unwind after a long day of exploring. The Pozos Termales are located within a short distance from the town center, and you can easily access them by car or on foot. Don't forget to bring your swimwear and a towel for a comfortable and relaxing experience.

Butterfly Haven

Panama city, 53rd St ave

If you're looking for a unique and magical experience in El Valle de Anton, don't miss the Butterfly Haven. This lovely attraction allows visitors to see hundreds of butterflies and moths in their natural habitat up close.

The Butterfly Haven is home to over 250 species of butterflies and moths, each with their own story and pattern. This is a perfect activity for nature enthusiasts, families, and those who are seeking a peaceful and calming atmosphere.

The knowledgeable and friendly staff at the Butterfly Haven will guide you through the different exhibits and provide you with interesting facts about the butterflies and moths. Don't forget to bring your camera to capture the colorful and stunning beauty of these creatures! A visit to the Butterfly Haven is an experience you won't forget.

Horseback riding

Panama city, Calle 50 - Casa La Guama

Exploring the beautiful El Valle de Anton in Central America can be a memorable experience, especially when you ride horses or zip-line through the lush green forest. There are several options to choose from, including guided tours and solo trips.

You'll have the opportunity to see panoramic views of the surrounding mountains and valleys while getting up close and personal with the local flora and fauna.

Horseback riding is perfect for those who want to take a leisurely pace and explore the area at a slower speed, while zip-lining offers an adrenaline rush as you soar through the forest canopy. Both activities are great for adventure seekers and nature lovers alike.
