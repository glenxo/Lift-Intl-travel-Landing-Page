All you need to know about
Coronado



Coronado, located on the Pacific coast of Panama, is a hidden gem
that is becoming increasingly popular with tourists and expats alike.
With its stunning beaches, lush vegetation, and welcoming
community, it's no wonder why people are flocking
to this tropical paradise. One of the main attractions of Coronado is its pristine beaches.

With warm, crystal-clear waters and soft white sand, these beaches
are perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and water
sports like surfing and paddleboarding. And with the year-round
tropical climate, you can enjoy the beach any time of year.
But there's more to Coronado than just its beaches.
The area is also home to a vibrant expat community,
with many retirees and digital nomads choosing to make
Coronado their permanent home. This has led to a
thriving restaurant scene, with a
range of international and local cuisine available.

If you're looking for outdoor activities, Coronado has plenty to offer.
Take a hike through the surrounding mountains, go birdwatching in the nearby
wetlands, or try your hand at deep-sea fishing in the Pacific Ocean.
So if you're looking for a tropical paradise
with a welcoming community,
stunning beaches, and plenty of activities,
look no further than Coronado in Panama.
