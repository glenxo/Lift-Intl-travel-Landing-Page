All you need to know about
Coronado

Coronado, located on the Pacific coast of Panama, is a hidden gem
that is becoming increasingly popular with tourists and expats alike.
With its stunning beaches, lush vegetation, and welcoming
community, it's no wonder why people are flocking
to this tropical paradise. One of the main attractions of Coronado is its pristine beaches.

With warm, crystal-clear waters and soft white sand, these beaches
are perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and water
sports like surfing and paddleboarding. And with the year-round
tropical climate, you can enjoy the beach any time of year.
But there's more to Coronado than just its beaches.
The area is also home to a vibrant expat community,
with many retirees and digital nomads choosing to make
Coronado their permanent home. This has led to a
thriving restaurant scene, with a
range of international and local cuisine available.

If you're looking for outdoor activities, Coronado has plenty to offer.
Take a hike through the surrounding mountains, go birdwatching in the nearby
wetlands, or try your hand at deep-sea fishing in the Pacific Ocean.
So if you're looking for a tropical paradise
with a welcoming community,
stunning beaches, and plenty of activities,
look no further than Coronado in Panama.

Are you an avid fisherman?

Looking for your next big catch? Then you won't want to miss the opportunity to go fishing for peacock bass on Gatun Lake! This popular sport fish is found only in Central and South America,

making it a unique and exciting challenge for anglers of all levels.

Our experienced guides will take you to the best spots on the lake to find these beautiful and hard-fighting fish, and provide all the equipment you need for a successful trip. So pack your tackle box and get ready for a day of fishing you'll never forget with Lift-Intl!

Soberania National Park

Looking for a way to immerse yourself in the natural beauty of Panama? Look no further than the Soberania National Park, located just a short distance from Gamboa. With its lush rainforest, winding rivers, and stunning views, this park is a must-see for any nature lover.

Take a hike on one of the many trails, keeping an eye out for colorful birds and exotic wildlife along the way.

Or simply relax and soak up the peaceful atmosphere of the forest. With so much natural beauty to explore, a visit to the Soberania National Park is an experience you won't soon forget! Let Lift-Intl take you on a guided tour and discover the wonders of this amazing national park.
