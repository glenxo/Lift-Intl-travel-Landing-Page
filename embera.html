Embera Experience

120 x person

PTY 507, Panama Canal Riviere - Chagres River

+ $25 after the 3rd · aprox.
3 hour up to 4hrs

"Step into the enchanting realm of the rainforest and uncover the vibrant culture of the Emberá Tribe.

Immerse yourself in age-old rituals, savor authentic cuisine, and create unforgettable memories amidst the lush surroundings.

Experience the warmth and hospitality of this authentic Indian Village as the natives warmly welcome you to share in their cultural heritage.

From traditional crafts to mesmerizing dances, discover the beauty of their people and the pristine environment that surrounds them."



Here's what to anticipate during this experience, guided by us.

"Let us begin your journey by picking you up from your preferred location
and whisking you away in our private transportation to experience
the unparalleled warmth and hospitality of this authentic Indian Village.

Embark on a scenic 30-45 minute river-up motor-propelled Indian canoe trip,
where the natives eagerly await to share their rich cultural heritage with you.

From immersing yourself in traditional crafts to being mesmerized by captivating dances,
prepare to delve into the beauty of their community and the pristine environment that envelops
them. Your visit promises to be a wonderful experience,
and we'll ensure your safe return to your place of stay".
