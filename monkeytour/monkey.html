All you need to know about Casco Viejo

Casco Viejo, also known as Casco Antiguo, is a historic neighborhood located
in the heart of Panama City, Central America. This UNESCO World Heritage site
is famous for its well-preserved colonial-era architecture, vibrant street life,
and rich cultural heritage. Visitors can explore the narrow cobblestone streets
and admire the colorful colonial buildings, many of which have been beautifully restored.
Casco Viejo is also home to numerous museums, art galleries, and cultural centers,
offering a glimpse into the history and culture of Panama.
In the evenings, the neighborhood comes alive with music
and street food, making it a popular destination for locals and tourists alike.
Whether you're interested in history, architecture, or simply soaking up
local culture, Casco Viejo is a must-visit destination in Panama City.
