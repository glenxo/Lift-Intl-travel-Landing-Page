All you need to know about
Soberanía Park & Gamboa



Gamboa is a small town located in the central part of Panama, known for its proximity to the stunning Gatun Lake. This man-made lake was created as a part of the Panama Canal construction, and has since become a popular tourist destination for its natural beauty and unique wildlife.

The Gatun Lake is home to a diverse range of flora and fauna, including crocodiles, monkeys, and various species of birds. Visitors can take a boat tour around the lake to see these creatures up close, or go fishing for peacock bass, a popular sport fish found only in Central and South America.

In addition to its natural attractions, Gamboa also offers a variety of activities for adventure-seekers, such as ziplining, hiking, and kayaking. The Soberania National Park is located nearby and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding rainforest.

If you're looking for a unique and unforgettable experience in Central America, a visit to Gamboa and the Gatun Lake is a must. Whether you're a nature enthusiast, an adventure junkie, or simply seeking a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life, this hidden gem has something to offer everyone.

So pack your bags and head to Gamboa, Panama for an unforgettable experience that will leave you with memories to last a lifetime.
