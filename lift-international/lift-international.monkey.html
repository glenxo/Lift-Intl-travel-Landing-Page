The Monkeys!

Panama Canal Riviere - Gamboa

At Lift-Intl, we take pride in offering unique travel experiences that you won't find anywhere else. When you visit the Gatun Lake with us, not only will you get to see the stunning wildlife that calls it home,

but you'll also have the opportunity to interact with some of the area's most beloved creatures - the monkeys! Our expert guides know just where to take you to find these playful primates, and will make sure you have a safe and enjoyable encounter.

Whether you're a wildlife enthusiast or just looking for a fun adventure, our monkey encounters on Gatun Lake are an experience you won't want to miss!.

Are you an avid fisherman?

Panama Canal Riviere - Gatun Lake

Looking for your next big catch? Then you won't want to miss the opportunity to go fishing for peacock bass on Gatun Lake! This popular sport fish is found only in Central and South America,

making it a unique and exciting challenge for anglers of all levels.

Our experienced guides will take you to the best spots on the lake to find these beautiful and hard-fighting fish, and provide all the equipment you need for a successful trip. So pack your tackle box and get ready for a day of fishing you'll never forget with Lift-Intl!

Rainforest Canopy

Panama city, 53rd St ave

Are you an adventure-seeker looking for an adrenaline rush? Then ziplining through the rainforest canopy in Gamboa is the perfect activity for you! Our expert guides will take you on a thrilling ride high above the trees, giving you a unique perspective on the stunning natural beauty of the area.

You'll feel the wind rushing through your hair as you soar through the air, and the rush of excitement as you land on each platform. Whether you're an experienced zipliner or a first-timer, our zipline adventure is sure to be a highlight of your trip to Panama. So book your adventure with Lift-Intl today and get ready to fly!

Gamboa Rainforest Resort

Panama city, Calle 50 - Casa La Guama

Looking for a luxurious getaway in the heart of the rainforest? Look no further than the Gamboa Rainforest Resort! Our resort offers spacious and comfortable rooms with stunning views of the surrounding jungle, as well as a range of amenities to make your stay as comfortable as possible.

Take a dip in our infinity pool, relax in our spa, or enjoy a delicious meal at one of our on-site restaurants. And with so many activities and attractions nearby, including boat tours, ziplining, and wildlife encounters, you'll never run out of things to do.

So book your stay with Lift-Intl today and experience the ultimate in luxury rainforest accommodations!

Soberania National Park

Panama city, 53rd St ave

Soberania National Park, located just a short distance from Gamboa. With its lush rainforest, winding rivers, and stunning views, this park is a must-see for any nature lover.

Take a hike on one of the many trails, keeping an eye out for colorful birds and exotic wildlife along the way.

Or simply relax and soak up the peaceful atmosphere of the forest. With so much natural beauty to explore, a visit to the Soberania National Park is an experience you won't soon forget! Let Lift-Intl take you on a guided tour and discover the wonders of this amazing national park.
